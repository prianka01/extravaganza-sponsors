Abha Bakaya

She started off as a print journalist at Business Today back in 2002 before embarking on a prolific television and online media career. Harishankar Karaunanidhi

Harishankar Karaunanidhi is the CTO & co-founder at HackerRank. Chennai born, Hari graduated from NIT Trichy in 2008. Initially enrolled for Electrical Engineering, Hari took interest in Computer Science in initial days of his college life and changed his department to CSE. He interned at Novell and was also selected for Google Summer of Code, under Firefox Organization. He worked at IBM, for a year as software engineer. Hari Karunanidhi and Vivek Ravisankar founded HackerRank in August 2009, with a vision to revolutionize the engineering recruitment process. HackerRank is a Y Combinator-backed company, and was the first Indian company accepted into Y Combinator start-up accelerator program. The start-up has raised more than 58 million USD in funding. HackerRank is a technological company that focuses on competitive programming challenges. It helps companies hire skilled developers. It serves as hiring platform for over1,000 companies. Its notable enterprise customers include Amazon, EA sports, Facebook, Twitter, Walmart among others. Hari specializes in programming, architecture and scaling. He was responsible for product delivery and management of HackerRank and he currently heads the India Office.

Anu Meena is the founder of AgroWave India,a startup that uses technology to bridge the wide gap between farmers and businesses in Indian Markets.
